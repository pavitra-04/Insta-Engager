# Insta-Engager

**👋 An AI Instagram profile analyser tool that suggest content strategies for higher engagement.**

## Description: What is it?

*Our product is an AI-powered platform that analyzes Instagram profiles and engagement data to recommend content strategies that help creators increase their reach and engagement.*

## Problem: What problem is this solving?

*This product solves the problem of creators not knowing what type of content performs best by providing data-driven insights and recommendations to improve engagement.*

## Why: How do we know this is a real problem and worth solving?

*Millions of content creators struggle to grow their reach and engagement due to a lack of data-driven insights about what content performs best on social media platforms.*

## **Goals**

1. **Increase engagement metrics** such as likes, comments, shares, and views through smart content recommendations.
2. **Help creators stay updated with trends** and feel more confident about what content to post next.
